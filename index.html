About

Interested by the short life span of contemporary constructions and objects, I have made this the main subject of my work.
Sometimes abstract, sometimes realistic, they bear witness to a world where obsolescence seems to reign supreme.

Inspired by artists such as Zao Wou-Ki or Surata Bajaj for their sense of composition and treatment of light, I began my apprenticeship in Grenoble with Catherine Harley and continue in Geneva with Joanis Andino.

Of all the art techniques discovered during these years, oil painting remains my favourite medium.
With a brush or a spatula, it allows me to construct and deconstruct rich and powerful paintings.
